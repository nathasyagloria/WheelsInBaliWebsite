Rental Requirements

For Companies

  • Copy of Business Permit (SIUP) and NPWP
  • Copy of Deed of Establishment
  • Copy of Company Registration Certificate (TDP)
  • Company Domicile Certificate
  • Legalization Letter from the Ministry of Law and Human Rights
  • Copy of Director's and Responsible Officer's ID card (KTP)
  • Authorization Letter if not represented by the company director
  • Copy of Driver's License (SIM)
  • On-site survey of company address

For Individuals

  • Copy of House Ownership Certificate or Residential Agreement
  • Copy of Land and Building Tax (PBB)
  • Copy of Tax ID Number (NPWP)
  • Copy of ID card (KTP/KITAS) of renter and guarantor
  • Copy of Family Card (KK)
  • Copy of Passport
  • Copy of Driver's License (SIM)
  • Copy of Work ID
  • Business Card
  • Guarantor (if necessary)
  • Proof of safe and suitable parking space
