About Us

At WheelsInBali, we believe that exploring Bali should be easy, affordable, and unforgettable. Founded in 2024, our platform connects you with the best car and motorbike rentals across the island. Whether you're chasing waterfalls or beach sunsets, we’ve got the perfect ride to take you there.

Our commitment is simple — safe, clean, and budget-friendly transportation that gives you the freedom to travel like a local. We partner only with verified, trusted local vendors to ensure your journey is worry-free.

Rental Requirements

For Companies

  • Copy of Business Permit (SIUP) and NPWP
  • Copy of Deed of Establishment
  • Copy of Company Registration Certificate (TDP)
  • Company Domicile Certificate
  • Legalization Letter from the Ministry of Law and Human Rights
  • Copy of Director's and Responsible Officer's ID card (KTP)
  • Authorization Letter if not represented by the company director
  • Copy of Driver's License (SIM)
  • On-site survey of company address

For Individuals

  • Copy of House Ownership Certificate or Residential Agreement
  • Copy of Land and Building Tax (PBB)
  • Copy of Tax ID Number (NPWP)
  • Copy of ID card (KTP/KITAS) of renter and guarantor
  • Copy of Family Card (KK)
  • Copy of Passport
  • Copy of Driver's License (SIM)
  • Copy of Work ID
  • Business Card
  • Guarantor (if necessary)
  • Proof of safe and suitable parking space

Important Notes

  • Survey process must be completed at least 3 days before the rental date for document verification.
  • A refundable security deposit is required as insurance and will be returned at the end of the rental.
  • Vehicles must only be driven by individuals with a valid Indonesian or International Driver's License.
  • All documents must be photocopies. The original documents must be shown during the survey for validation.
  • Do not leave the vehicle's STNK, keys, or parking tickets unattended inside the vehicle.
  • Vehicle replacement is not available if damage or accidents occur due to user negligence.
  • All renters and additional drivers must be aged between 21–65 years for standard cars and 25–65 years for premium vehicles.
  • Self-drive rentals require renters to monitor regular vehicle maintenance.
  • Survey results are independently assessed and final. If rejected, no further clarification will be provided.
  • Currently, motorcycle rentals are only available for corporate clients.
  • Motorcycle rental deposit is IDR 1,000,000 per unit. Refunds are processed within 3 working days after return and inspection.
